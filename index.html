Cristiano Ronaldo

The Unstoppable

Here's a time line of Cristiano Ronaldo's life:

  • 1985 - Born in Madeira, Portugal
  • 1995 - Signs with Nacional and subsequently Sporting CP
  • 2002 - Scores brace in league debut
  • 2003 - Signed by Manchester United
  • 2003 - Scores first goal against Portsmouth
  • 2004 - Receives red card for the first time
  • 2007 - Scores first Champions League goal
  • 2007 - Scores first hat-trick
  • 2007 - Captains Portugal for the first time
  • 2008 - Captains United for the first time
  • 2008 - Receives Ballon d'Or
  • 2009 - Leaves Manchester United to join Real Madrid
  • 2010 - Scores first hat-trick for Real Madrid
  • 2012 - Wins La Liga with Real Madrid
  • 2014 - Becomes Portugal's record goalscorer
  • 2014 - Wins Champions League with Real Madrid
  • 2015 - Wins third Ballon d'Or
  • 2015 - Becomes Real Madrid's all-time record goalscorer
